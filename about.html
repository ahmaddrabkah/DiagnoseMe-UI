Our Idea

DiagnoseMe is the first world wide web application that bring the hospital into your hands.

In our daily lives, we intend to go to hospitals to get diagnosis and prescriptions from doctors, but it is not as easy as that. You need to wait in line all day just to schedule an appointment; it may take from 1 week to months, but what if you can get your diagnosis using your phone or your computer without the need of going to a hospital or a doctor clinic? Would not that be easier? Therefore, we thought of our idea to make it easier for patients to get their diagnosis; it is a web application where you can enter your medical information like age, gender, height, calories, blood pressure, etc. Then it can predict if you have a certain disease or not using machine learning.

Our Vision

To provide online platform that can be used the patients from any age and gender around the world with the ability to diagnose their diseases at any time from anywhere.

Our Mission

To make patients life easier by making the diagnosis operations available at anytime and anywhere.
